XCOM 2 Update Makes Multiple Fixes, Improves Performance

Certain save file bugs have also been fixed.

XCOM 2 received an update today that fixes issues causing crashes and improves performance. Additionally, players who have been prevented from continuing their saved games because of bugs may now be able to do so.2K community manager Dave Hinkle posted the update announcement on XCOM 2's Steam page today. He said that players who couldn't progress past the Resistance Communications research part of the tutorial will now be able to after today's patch. He also noted that adjustments have been made to Texture Streaming to assist with performance.
